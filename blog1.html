The Journey to Full Stack: Why RMF Wizard Matters

Starting my journey into Full Stack development wasn't just about learning code; it was about solving real-world problems. Coming from a cybersecurity background, I realized that the Risk Management Framework (RMF) process is incredibly manual and tedious.

The Problem

Cybersecurity professionals spend hours navigating spreadsheets and PDF controls. I asked myself: "Why isn't there a wizard for this?"

The Solution

That is where the idea for RMF Wizard was born. By combining my new skills in javascript, html, css and with my experience in DoD doctrines, I am building a tool that automates the compliance architecture.

"Automation is the ultimate form of security efficiency."

Stay tuned as I move from the Design phase into the Development phase next month!
